Pitch Your Experiment

Experimental philosophy brings philosophy out of the armchair and into the real world by incorporating the intuitions of laypeople into investigations. D-Phi takes it a step further. We believe laypeople should have the opportunity to contribute to the production of knowledge. The academy should be open to those outside of it, but not at the expense of abandoning scientific rigor. This is achieved through cooperation between laypeople and specialists. The Your Idea Area facilitates this cooperation. Anyone can propose an idea for experimental research. Ideas selected by our team will receive all the technical support needed to be put into practice. We believe this section will increase our lab's ability to think outside the bubble!
