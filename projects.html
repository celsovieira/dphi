Cross-Cultural Epistemology

Principal Investigator: Dr. [Name]

Description: Examining whether intuitions about knowledge vary across cultures and what this means for epistemological theories. We're testing Gettier cases, epistemic closure principles, and contextualist claims in diverse cultural contexts.

Methods: Surveys in Portuguese, English, and Spanish; cross-cultural statistical analysis

Status: Ongoing

Collaborators: International x-phi network

Free Will, Determinism, and Moral Responsibility

Principal Investigator: [Researcher Name]

Description: Exploring folk beliefs about free will, responsibility, and the compatibility of determinism with moral responsibility. This project examines whether laypeople are natural compatibilists or incompatibilists.

Methods: Vignette studies, scenario manipulation

Status: Planning stage

The Experience Machine: Revisiting Nozick

Principal Investigator: [Researcher Name]

Description: An empirical investigation of people's responses to Nozick's experience machine thought experiment. We're testing whether framing effects, demographic factors, and gaming experience influence acceptance of the machine.

Methods: Online experiments with multiple conditions

Status: Analysis phase
